What happens when you hit your daily limit?

How should the app behave when all credits are used up for the day?

A

Hard Block

Disable the chat input and analysis buttons entirely. Show a clear message: "You've used all your credits for today. Resets at midnight." No way around it.

B

Soft Warning + Block

Show a warning toast when approaching the limit (e.g. 2 credits left). Then hard block when fully exhausted with a friendly message and link to adjust the limit in settings.

C

Warning Only (No Block)

Show a warning when the limit is reached but still allow interactions. The limit acts as a budget reminder, not a hard stop. Good for "No Limit" users who still want awareness.
